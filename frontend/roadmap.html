Career Roadmap

Freelance Web Developer

  • Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Practice building websites
  • Create a portfolio of your work
  • Understand freelance platforms
  • Work with clients and deliver projects

Project Manager

  • Learn time management and planning
  • Understand basic project tools (like Trello)
  • Practice team communication
  • Get familiar with budgeting
  • Lead small projects to gain experience

Social Worker

  • Improve communication and empathy skills
  • Learn about social issues and rights
  • Get training in counseling basics
  • Volunteer with community organizations
  • Pursue certifications or degrees in social work

Professional Athlete

  • Choose a sport you enjoy and commit to it
  • Train regularly and track progress
  • Join local teams or clubs
  • Work with a coach or mentor
  • Compete and gain recognition

Teacher

  • Identify subjects you like teaching
  • Practice explaining ideas clearly
  • Help others learn in study groups
  • Take teaching assistant roles
  • Pursue formal education or certification

Engineer

  • Explore areas: mechanical, civil, computer, etc.
  • Build basic math and science skills
  • Join STEM clubs or online courses
  • Create simple projects at home
  • Plan for a degree in engineering field

Software Developer

  • Learn a programming language like Python
  • Build small apps or games
  • Understand problem-solving logic
  • Join coding communities for help
  • Work on real-world projects
